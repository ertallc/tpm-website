Our services

Thompson Property Management LLC offers full-service management for residential and commercial property owners across the Kansas City metropolitan area.

Leasing & marketing

Listing and advertising vacancies, coordinating showings, applicant screening, and preparing and renewing leases.

Tenant relations

A responsive point of contact for tenants by phone, email, and text for questions, requests, and notices.

Maintenance & repairs

Coordinating routine maintenance and repairs with trusted vendors and keeping everyone informed of scheduling.

Rent collection

Invoicing, payment reminders, and processing so owners receive funds reliably and on time.

Owner reporting

Clear, regular statements and updates that give owners a transparent view of their properties.

Lease & compliance notices

Timely notices regarding lease terms, renewals, inspections, and other property matters.

How we communicate
